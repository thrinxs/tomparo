# TomParo Job Marketplace Vision

## What TomParo Really Is

A complete AI-powered job marketplace connecting job seekers with employers, with intelligent matching, career coaching, and hiring tools.

## Ecosystem Overview

### For Job Seekers:

- Free CV analysis (with limits)
- Premium: Unlimited AI, job discovery, interview coaching
- Job matching from TomParo jobs + external sources
- 1-click apply
- Application tracking

### For Employers/Recruiters:

- Post jobs (free or paid)
- AI-ranked candidates
- Bulk CV screening
- Candidate database search
- Team collaboration

## Revenue Streams

1. **Consumer Premium** (₦5,000/mo)
2. **Recruiter Subscriptions** (₦5k - ₦150k/mo)
3. **Job Posting Fees** (₦5k - ₦50k/post)
4. **Affiliate Revenue** (courses, certs)
5. **Company Branding** (₦20k - ₦100k/mo)
