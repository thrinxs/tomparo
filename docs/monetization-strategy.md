# TomParo Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Consumer Premium (B2C)

- **Price:** ₦5,000/month or ₦50,000/year
- **Includes:** All AI features unlimited
- **Target:** Individual job seekers

### 2. Recruiter Subscriptions (B2B) - ALL INCLUSIVE

No hidden fees. No per-post charges.

| Plan       | Price/Mo | CVs/Mo    | Job Posts            | Perks       |

| Starter    | ₦5,000   | 20        | 3 active             | Basic       |
| Growth     | ₦10,000  | 50        | 10 active            | Analytics   |
| Business   | ₦30,000  | 200       | Unlimited            | Team        |
| Enterprise | ₦80,000  | 500       | Unlimited + Featured | Priority    |
| Scale      | ₦150,000 | 1,000     | Unlimited + Priority | Custom      |
| Custom     | Custom   | Unlimited | Everything           | White-glove |

### 3. Affiliate Revenue

- Course platforms (Coursera, Udemy, Codecademy)
- Certification programs (AWS, Google, Microsoft)
- 5-30% commission per sale
- Shown in skill gap recommendations

### 4. Advertising (Free Users Only)

#### Rules:

- ✅ Premium users see ZERO ads (their promise)
- ✅ Only career/education-relevant ads
- ✅ Never intrusive
- ✅ Maximum 3 ads per page

#### Where:

- Landing page (top banner)
- Dashboard (sidebar)
- Job feed (native ads)
- Between AI processing

#### Where NOT:

- Inside AI results
- Auth/signup forms
- Payment pages
- Interview coaching

#### Phase-in Strategy:

- Phase 1 (0-1000 users): NO ADS
- Phase 2 (1000-5000): Affiliate only
- Phase 3 (5000+): Curated ads
- Phase 4 (20k+): Direct deals + network

### 5. Company Branding (Future)

- Verified company profiles
- Employer branding pages
- ₦20,000 - ₦100,000/month

## Key Principles

1. **Simple pricing** (no hidden fees)
2. **Premium = Ad-free** (sacred promise)
3. **Value-based tiers** (bigger = more features)
4. **Free tier stays free** (with ads)
5. **Recruiter = All-inclusive** (no nickel-and-diming)
